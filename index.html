Hello, I am

Meixin Yuan

I am a Ph.D. student in Urban and Regional Planning at University of Michigan, Taubman College. I am also working on a Master of Science in Information at UMSI. I received my Master of Urban and Regional Planing at Taubman College and BS in Landscape Architecure from Northwest Agriculture and Forestry University in China.

I am passionate about urban informatics and I am consistently pursuing to answer urban questions with novel methods. My Ph.D. research focuses on Planning Supprt Systems and the intersection of urban planning and public health. For my dual degree in MSI, I am specialized in big data analytics.

I always want to try something new and never hesitate to share my knowledge and skills. You can find out more about my research experience , teaching experience, my publications, my design portfolios, and some knowledge-shraring technology blogs at this site.
